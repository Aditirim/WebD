About Me

Hello, I'm Maria , a professional photographer based in India.

Based in India, I am a passionate photographer who believes in capturing life as it happens—raw, real, and beautiful. With a camera in my hand and a love for storytelling in my heart, I’ve spent the last 12years turning fleeting moments into timeless photographs.
